Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I get it installed?

A technician will install the Roam at a local hub in your region. We will provide the address during the scheduling process.

How is the Roam device installed?

There is no drilling or cutting involved in installing a Roam. It uses quality roof racks to install the device on the roof of your vehicle.

How long do I have to keep it on?

You can have the Roam installed for as long or short as you would like. There is no minimum requirement.

How do I get paid?

Payments are sent Monthly every Monday.

Can I remove the Roam myself?

Drivers should not remove the Roam themselves. Only installation technicians are allowed to do so. Please reach out to Roam Ads to schedule a Roam uninstallation appointment.

Will it harm my battery?

No! The unit turns on when the car is on and switches to solar or in-house battery when the car turns off.

Do I get paid to refer my friends?

Yes! You will receive a $5 referral bonus for any referred drivers that receive the Roam and show ads for their first 50 Hours.

What happens if I don’t drive with the Roam for more than 7 consecutive days?

If you don’t drive with the Roam for longer than 7 consecutive days, please contact us to remove the display and it will be reinstalled whenever you want to return to the program.

Can the Roam go through a car wash?

Yes, though we do not recommend going through a machine wash with a top roller. A hand wash or side wash is okay.
